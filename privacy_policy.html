Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes Our policies and procedures on the collection, use and disclosure of Your information when You use the pdfreader application (the "Service") and tells You about Your privacy rights and how the law protects You.

1. Information Collection and Use

While using Our Service, We may collect certain information to provide and improve the Service.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

We do not require You to create an account or provide personally identifiable information (such as your name or email address) to use the core features of the PDF Reader. However, some third-party services used by the app may collect data as described below.

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically when using the Service. This may include information such as Your Device's Internet Protocol address (e.g. IP address), browser type, browser version, the pages of our Service that You visit, the time and date of Your visit, the time spent on those pages, unique device identifiers and other diagnostic data.

File and Storage Information

To provide its core functionality, the App requires access to Your device's storage to pick, read, and display PDF files.

  • Local Processing: All PDF processing and viewing (via Syncfusion PDF library) are performed locally on Your device. We do not upload Your documents to our servers.
  • File Picker: The App uses a file picker to allow You to select documents.

2. Third-Party Services and Advertising

Our Service uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify You.

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data (such as advertising IDs) to personalize ads and provide analytics.

Text-to-Speech (TTS)

The App uses flutter_tts for document narration. This service may rely on the underlying OS (Android/iOS) engine, which may have its own data processing policies.

3. Local Data Storage

The App uses a local database (sqflite) to store your preferences, bookmarks, or reading history. This data remains on Your device and is not transmitted to Us.

4. Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • Storage/Media: To read PDF files stored on your device.
  • Internet: To load advertisements via AdMob.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.
